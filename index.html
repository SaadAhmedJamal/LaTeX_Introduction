LaTeX

A document preparation system

  • A free software (Don't have to pay for using LaTeX, no license fees )
  • Most suitable for medium-to-large documents (technical or scientific)
  • Typesetting (Standardisation)
  • Makes us not to worry too much about the appearance



[1] https://www.latex-project.org/

Features

  • Typesetting
    • journal articles
    • technical reports
    • books
    • slide presentations
    • resume/CV
  • Fonts and formulas
    • Mathematical formulas
    • Complex Advanced typesetting (mathematics) | AMS-LaTeX.
    • Multi-lingual
    • Special fonts (PostScript or Metafont)
  • Control (esp. large documents)
    • sectioning
    • cross-references
    • tables
    • figures
  • Bibliography Automation (indexes and references)
  • Customisation
